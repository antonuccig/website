Research

My research project concerns the development of mathematical theories, algorithms, and software for the time-parallelisation of plasma physics simulations.

My supervisors are Prof Raphael Hauser from the Mathematical Institute, and Dr Debasmita Samaddar and Dr Stanislav Pamela from the Culham Centre for Fusion Energy (CCFE), the UK's national laboratory for fusion research.

Click here for further details on my current research.

Other research interests include:

  • numerical solutions of differential equations,
  • scientific and high-performance computing,
  • mathematical modelling,
  • artificial intelligence,
  • data science.

Note that this is by no means an exhaustive list. You are welcome to ask me about other areas as well.

Collaborations with industry

As a trainee at the Industrially Focused Mathematical Modelling (Centre for Doctoral Training), I have worked alongside industries on problems arising in commercial settings. Collaborations of this kind are often fruitful for both the industrial partner, who benefits from reduced efforts and costs, and the mathematician, who is challenged with interesting research questions.
